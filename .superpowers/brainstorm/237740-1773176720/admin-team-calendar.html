How it works

  • Green dot = 6+ hours of CRM activity (full day)
  • Yellow dot = Under 6 hours (partial day)
  • Red dot = Zero sessions recorded (absent)
  • Gray dot = Weekend (non-working day)
  • Orange ring = Today's cell, highlighted for quick reference
  • Hover any cell to see hours + session count in tooltip
  • Click any cell to open the Day Detail panel (next mockup)
  • Avg column on the right shows daily average hours for the month
  • Top summary bar shows team-wide aggregates
